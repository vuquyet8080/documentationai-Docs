Evidence-based guidelines for optimal patient outcomes.

|---------------|----------------|-----------|
| Application | Use minimal pressure | Reduces tissue trauma |
| Frequency | Follow product guidelines | Ensures safety and efficacy |
| Monitoring | Track patient response | Allows for adjustments |
